    Color Mode:

    RGB Color Mode

    There are two main color modes, being RGB and CMYK.

    While most of our printers actually print in CMYK, quality is maintained best when we receive RGB files and have our printers convert those files to CMYK.

    RGB stands for Red, Green and Blue, with all of the colors included in RGB files are created using a combination of red, green and blue light.

    Bleed:

    Application Area Size

    The Application Area Size is the size of the surface where you will be placing your vinyl lettering. For example, if you were looking to place your vinyl lettering on an 8ft wide by 8ft tall wall and wanted to see your cut vinyl at scale, you would change the Application Area Size to 8ft wide by 8ft tall.
    The actual size of your object is indicated by the width and height fields in the dialogue box that appears when an object is selected.
